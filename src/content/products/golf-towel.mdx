The MOSTY Premium Golf Cleaning Towel is your on-course companion for keeping clubs, balls, and hands clean. Made from ultra-absorbent microfiber with a textured waffle pattern, this tri-fold towel locks in moisture and scrubs away dirt effortlessly.

## Better Than a Standard Towel

Most golf towels are thin, non-absorbent, and fall apart after a few rounds. The MOSTY towel features:

- **Waffle-weave pattern** for superior scrubbing power
- **Tri-fold design** that gives you three clean surfaces per round
- **Durable stitching** that holds up to regular machine washing

## Clip & Go

The built-in carabiner clip attaches to any golf bag or push cart, so your towel is always within reach.
