The MOSTY Golf Club & Ball Cleaner Bag is the ultimate on-course cleaning solution. Designed with a waterproof inner liner and a scratch-free microfiber interior, this compact pouch lets you clean both your club faces and golf balls in seconds.

## Why It Matters

Dirty clubs mean less spin, less control, and worse scores. Pro golfers clean their clubs after every shot — and with the MOSTY Cleaner Bag, you can too, without slowing down play.

Just add 1/5 cup of water, attach it to your bag with the included carabiner clip, and enjoy pristine clubs throughout your entire round.

## Who It's For

Whether you're a weekend warrior or a competitive player, the MOSTY Cleaner Bag is designed for anyone who wants to play better golf with cleaner equipment.
