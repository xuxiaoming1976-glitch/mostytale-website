Keeping your clubs clean during a round isn't just about looking good — it directly impacts your performance. Dirty grooves reduce spin by up to 50%, which means less control on approach shots and around the greens.

## The Problem with Traditional Methods

Most golfers carry a damp towel and scrub their clubs before each shot. But towels dry out quickly on hot days, don't clean deep inside grooves, and can actually transfer grime between clubs.

## Step-by-Step On-Course Cleaning Routine

### 1. Pre-Round Setup
Add about 1/5 cup of water to your MOSTY cleaner bag and clip it to your bag. That's all the prep you need.

### 2. After Every Shot
Slide the club face into the bag, give it 2-3 gentle scrubs, and it's clean. The whole process takes about 10 seconds.

### 3. Between Holes
Use the ball side to clean your golf ball for better putting accuracy. Clean balls roll truer.

### 4. At the Turn
Refresh the water at the clubhouse if available. Fresh water cleans even better.

## Pro Tips for Cleaner Clubs

- Use warm water when available for superior cleaning
- Pair the cleaner bag with the MOSTY Golf Towel for drying
- Clean after every shot in wet conditions when mud is a factor
- Don't forget to clean your wedges — they need the most spin
